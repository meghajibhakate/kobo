  • VIDEOS 2. Receive Notifications

    Once you've subscribed to a bot, you'll receive notifications every time a new article is posted on the IDR website (for the English and Hindi bots) or when there is a new post or discussion on the Tech4Good forum (for the Tech4Good bot). You can choose to receive notifications in real-time or at a frequency that works for you.
